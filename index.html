How can I help?

In order to make this app a reality we need your support to show that people are interested in having such an app. In addition, we would like to know what healthcare providers you would want to see available. This could be your specific GP, a health service you find difficult to find or any information you may want about health service providers which you're not able to find.



To register your support for this app, just fill in the form below showing your interest and any healthcare provider you would want to be available on the app. If you also have the time to fill out this very short survey on your experience with healthcare seervices
